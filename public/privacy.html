Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026. We take your privacy seriously and handle your data with care.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account, place an order, or contact us:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Delivery addresses for order fulfillment
  • Order history and preferences
  • Messages sent through our support system

We automatically collect technical data including your session ID, device type, and browser information to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Improve our products and services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS with transport-layer encryption. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text). Authentication uses HTTP-only JWT cookies with SameSite=Strict and CSRF protection via HMAC-SHA256 signed tokens.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use an HTTP-only secure cookie (bloom_token) for authentication. Local storage is used for theme preferences, session metadata, and cart state. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be shared with delivery partners solely for order fulfillment, and with payment processors for transaction processing.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data through your profile page
  • Update or correct your information at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your order history

Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. Order records are kept for 3 years for tax and legal compliance. You can request account deletion at any time through your profile or by contacting support.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@bloom.ph or through our support page.
