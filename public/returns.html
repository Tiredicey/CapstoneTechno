Our Service Promise

We don't just deliver flowers. We deliver on our word. If something goes wrong, here's exactly what we do.

🌹 Freshness Guarantee

Every bouquet is cold-chain handled at 0–4°C from harvest to delivery. If your flowers arrive wilted or damaged, we'll remake the arrangement or issue a full refund within 24 hours. No questions, no negotiations.

📸 Photo Proof

We photograph every delivery at the recipient's location with a timestamp. If the delivered bouquet doesn't match what was quoted at checkout, we'll either remake or refund — your choice.

⏰ On-Time Delivery

Your chosen 1-hour delivery window is a commitment. If we deliver outside the agreed window, we'll credit ₱500 to your account automatically. Evening slots (6–9 PM) included.

💬 Responsive Support

Reach us via live chat, email, or Viber. Our support team responds within 2 hours during operating hours (8 AM–8 PM PHT, 7 days a week). Urgent delivery issues are escalated immediately.

↩️ Hassle-Free Resolution

If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll work with you to find a resolution — remake, partial refund, or full refund. We handle the return logistics if needed.

🔒 Secure Transactions

All payments are processed over HTTPS with transport-layer encryption. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Sessions use HTTP-only JWT cookies with CSRF protection. Your data is never shared with third parties.
