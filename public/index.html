Receipts

Claims, with sources. No handwaving.

0–4°C
Postharvest cold-chain target
Cut flowers held in this range lose significantly less turgor and microbial load than ambient handling, extending vase life.
Source: UC Davis Postharvest — Cut Flower Recommendations
1,500 m+
Benguet growing altitude
La Trinidad and surrounding Benguet municipalities sit in a cool-weather zone ideal for rose, chrysanthemum, and aster cultivation year-round.
Source: PAGASA Climate Divisions · DA-BPI
WCAG 2.1
Accessibility conformance target
Level AA: perceivable, operable, understandable, robust. Keyboard parity, 4.5:1 text contrast, reduced-motion honored, ARIA labels on every interactive element.
Source: W3C Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1
SDG 17
Partnership-first sourcing
We buy direct from DA-BPI registered cooperatives, eliminating middlemen. Grower share of final price is disclosed on every bouquet card.
Source: UN Sustainable Development Goal 17
HTTPS + JWT
Account + order security
Transport encrypted end-to-end. Passwords hashed with bcrypt (per project auth middleware). JWT sessions with HTTP-only auth headers.
Source: OWASP authentication cheat sheet · OWASP
Photo proof
Delivery verification
Every delivery closes with a timestamped photo at the recipient's door or hand. If it doesn't match the quoted bouquet, we remake or refund.
