Our promise

What we care about when handling your order.

Four things we designed around from day one: keeping flowers fresh, delivering on time, making the site usable for everyone, and being honest about what we can do.

Keeping flowers fresh

We follow temperature handling practices recommended by UC Davis postharvest research to help your flowers last longer after delivery.

You pick the delivery time

At checkout, choose a delivery window that works for you — including evening slots. We do our best to deliver within that window.

Designed for everyone

This site works with keyboards, screen readers, and respects reduced-motion preferences. We test against WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards.

We show you, not just tell you

When your order is delivered, we send a photo so you can see it arrived safely. If something doesn't look right, reach out and we'll help fix it.

Transparency

See where your flowers come from.

We work with growers near Lipa City, Batangas. Each order includes a QR code you can scan to see its journey — from harvest to your door — with live weather data from Windy.com for each farm location.

How we built this

What we used, where we learned it, and why it matters.

0–4°C
Temperature-aware handling
Flowers last longer when kept cool. We follow the 0–4°C recommended range from UC Davis postharvest research to help your arrangement stay fresh.
Source: UC Davis Postharvest Technology Center
BSIT
Student-built from scratch
Every line of code, every design decision, and every feature on this platform was built by BSIT students at STI College Lipa as a capstone project.
Source: STI College Lipa · BSIT Program
WCAG 2.1
Accessible to more people
We target Level AA — meaning the site is designed to be usable by people with visual, motor, and cognitive differences. That includes keyboard navigation, readable contrast, and screen reader support.
Source: W3C Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1
SDG 17
Partnerships for the goals
Bloom connects BSIT students with local flower growers in Lipa City and open-source tools like Windy.com and Web Crypto API — a student-community-technology partnership aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 17.
Source: UN SDG 17 · STI Lipa
HTTPS + JWT
Your data stays protected
All connections use HTTPS encryption. Your password is hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plain text). Login sessions use secure, HTTP-only tokens that can't be accessed by scripts.
Source: OWASP authentication cheat sheet · OWASP
Photo proof
You'll see it when it arrives
We take a photo when your order is delivered so you know it got there safely. If anything looks off, let us know and we'll work with you to make it right.
Policy: Bloom Service Promise
