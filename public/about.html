Our Values

What guides every decision.

❄️
Freshness First

Every bouquet follows UC Davis postharvest cold-chain guidelines. 0–4°C from harvest to handoff extends vase life up to 2x.

♿
Accessibility by Default

WCAG 2.1 AA conformance target. Keyboard navigation, screen reader labels, reduced motion support, and 4.5:1 contrast ratios.

📸
Honest Delivery

Timestamped photo proof at the recipient's door. If the bouquet doesn't match what we quoted, we remake or refund.
